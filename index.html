PROBLEM STATEMENT

OBJECTIVES

Indoor air pollution is regularly ranked among the world's 
top five health  risks that could be caused by LPG leaks,
household chemicals (such as detergents,  perfumes,paints, etc.)
that raise toxicity.

To detect the indoor air quality along with its concentration and
to give the user a warning.

Being aware of the hazardous events taking place in houses and
building is a problem especially when the individual is not at
that particular location or away from the particular location.

To detect the concentration of LPG, CO and smoke and trigger a
warning to the user.

Without such knowledge, people could have breathing problems
and many others such as watery eyes, nausea, headaches.

To detect various fire hazards using image processing and alert
both user and fire department to reduce further damage.



To represent all the data collected in real-time graphs,
so that the user can be aware of the real-time events
where this system is implemented.
